# A Portable Guitar Neck Trainer

## Phase 1: Prototype
- A mobile app to train chords, chord switching, and fingering patterns
- A touchscreen app acts as the guitar neck
- The app assumes the right orientation for the guitar neck based on user's preference
- Has built in chords and fingering patterns
- Shows dots on the screen to indicate the right finger positions.
- User holds the screen like a guitar neck and places the fingers on the right position to play the chord
- The app can be used to train chords and fingering patterns
- The app trains the user's fingers and muscles to accurately and quickly play the chords


## Phase 2: Music Library
- Allow the user to search for songs and their chords, and display them on the screen 
- Play the song as the user plays the chord on the screen
- Also allow the app to act as a display to show the chords and progression so the user can practice playing by following the tabs


## Phase 3: Custom Uploads
- Allow the user to upload their own custom chords and fingering patterns
